Services

Switch Construction can support you through every stage of the construction process. From framing to finish, we handle both large and small jobs with the same dedication to quality and craftsmanship.

Framing

Setting the standard for a high-end, quality finish begins with precise framing. We provide a full range of framing services for residential and commercial projects. Our experts build frames with exceptional care, ensuring structural integrity while accommodating design specifications. All framing jobs both big and small can be accommodated, from single room additions to complete building structures.

Decking

A deck can be a great addition to any house, adding extra usable space and significant appeal to a home. We have installed and maintained countless decks throughout the Bay Area using various materials including traditional wood, composite, and exotic hardwoods. Our team ensures proper drainage, structural stability, and beautiful finishes that withstand the elements while complementing your home's architecture.

Carpentry

Our services include all aspects of woodworking in homebuilding and renovation. From custom cabinetry and built-ins to crown molding and wainscoting, our craftsmen bring decades of experience to every project. We take pride in creating unique, functional, and beautiful wood elements that elevate your space and stand the test of time. Each piece is meticulously planned and executed with superior attention to detail.

Window Installation

We provide full-service window installations for both new construction and replacement projects. Our team is experienced with various window styles and manufacturers, ensuring proper fitting, sealing, and finishing for optimal energy efficiency and aesthetic appeal. We carefully manage each installation to minimize disruption while maximizing light, view, and ventilation according to your specific needs and preferences.

Door Installation

We provide complete door installation services for both interior and exterior applications. From standard pre-hung doors to custom solid wood entries, pocket doors, and sliding systems, our team handles every aspect of the installation process. We ensure proper operation, security, and weather sealing while paying careful attention to hardware selection, trim details, and finishing touches that complete the look and functionality of your doorways.

Bar Specialist

We have designed, renovated, and built many bars throughout the Bay Area, from commercial establishments to high-end residential entertainment spaces. Our specialized knowledge includes proper dimensions, plumbing integration, lighting, and the unique woodworking techniques required for bar construction. We collaborate closely with owners and designers to create functional, beautiful spaces that become the centerpiece of any gathering while meeting all commercial code requirements.
